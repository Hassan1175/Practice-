What is Swami???

Students Welfare Association Mianwali (SWAMI) was established in 2010 to guide students in carrier selection, university level admission, scholarship opportunities and financial support. The ideology of the association roots back to fellow professionals and student community which could acquire education themselves at various universities in Pakistan and abroad, with and objective to promote education in Mianwali and country as a whole. The major events and objectives of association include guidance seminars, self-assessment tests, introductory lectures, and knowledge sharing among the members in gathering.

Fundamental Objectives of Association:
  • Promotion of mutual aid, help and support among the members
  • Sharing information about admission procedures and scholarship opportunities in different colleges and universities of various fields
  • To broaden the view of students about various educational fields which can help them in choosing the field suitable for them
  • Guidance about social, educational and professional issues
  • To help the students up-to maximum possible limit through various sources
